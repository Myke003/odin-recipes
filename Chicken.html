Chicken and Vegetable Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into thin strips
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 1/2 cup snow peas
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, minced
  • Cooked rice for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, mix together soy sauce, cornstarch, and ginger to make the sauce. Set aside.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat.
  3. Add the chicken strips and stir-fry until they're no longer pink. Remove them from the pan.
  4. In the same pan, add the minced garlic, bell pepper, broccoli, carrot, and snow peas. Stir-fry for a few minutes until the vegetables are tender but still crisp.
  5. Return the cooked chicken to the pan, and pour the sauce over everything. Stir and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  6. Serve the stir-fry over cooked rice.
